# 1. Slice the World Map
vips dzsave images/world_map.png images/tiles_world --layout google --suffix .png

# 2. Slice the Hafngard City Map
vips dzsave images/hafngard.png images/tiles_hafngard --layout google --suffix .png

# 3. Slice the Thornreach District Map
vips dzsave images/thornreach_district.png images/tiles_thornreach --layout google --suffix .png

What these parameters mean:
dzsave: The deep-zoom saving utility inside libvips.

--layout google: Generates folders in the z/x/y.png format, which is the default structure Leaflet uses.

--suffix .png: Forces the sliced tile pieces to save as .png images.

After running these, you will see three new folders inside your images/ directory: tiles_world, tiles_hafngard, and tiles_thornreach.
